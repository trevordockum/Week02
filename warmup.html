Warm up matches before ranked.. AKA "SMURFING"

Last but not least a very pivital part in the process of getting ready to que high GC lobbies is to first hop over on an alternate account for some lesiure warm up games. This "alt" account can be one of many that you have created to just have at your finger tips, when you want to play lower ranked players and not have to worry about messing up your main account's rank for the season. Have access to a few different "alt" accounts is necessary! If one gets too high of a rank for your liking you can hop over to another one and rinse and repeat.
